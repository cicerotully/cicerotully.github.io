All this is to say that I have written several scripts and other automations that I'll share in due time, bringing me on to my next point...

I will be unveiling my digital marketing blog this quarter and it'll be at this address: www.scriptwisemarketer.co.uk (the link doesn't take you anywhere, so hold your horses for now).
